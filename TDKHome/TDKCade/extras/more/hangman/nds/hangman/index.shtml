About

Guess the hidden word before the man hangs. Enter letters by touching them with the stylus. At the end, use the 'New game' button to start a new game.

Let me know your favourite difficult Hangman words.

This game is designed to show the gallows and word on the top screen, and the letter list, guesses and new game button on the bottom screen. Align now.
